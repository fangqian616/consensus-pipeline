## 镜头1[0-4s·追逐建立] | 4秒视频

**景别**：大远景
**镜头**：27mm T4.0深景深，马车右后上方3/4视角俯瞰，纵深构图，手持摄影机微幅晃动
**打光**：暖金色（5000K）侧逆光，马车金色轮廓光，体积光穿透沙尘，沙虫逆光剪影
**画面**：马车右后上方3/4俯瞰。前景左侧，沙虫巨口微张追赶马车，逆光剪影压迫极强。中景偏右，马车高速疾驰，棕色马全力冲刺四肢完全伸展，运动模糊，沙尘暴尾迹。车尾窗口奥梵剪影可辨，面朝后方持弩炮。深处车头，莉雅赶马背影，银发狂风后扬。前方地平线暖金夕阳
**站位**：沙虫@前景左侧大；马车@中景偏右纵深；奥梵@车尾窗口；莉雅@车头背影
**背景虚化**：深景深全画面清晰

### 正面提示词

Cinematic wide shot, 3/4 rear high angle looking down from behind and to the right of a horse-drawn wagon, vast desert at golden hour. A massive sandworm pursues the wagon from left foreground. The sandworm fills the left foreground — enormous segmented body and circular maw in silhouette against golden backlight, gaining ground rapidly. The wagon with a brown horse at full gallop speeds across mid-ground right — horse legs at full extension, motion blur on hooves and wheels, massive dust clouds kicked up behind. At the wagon's rear window, a girl figure faces backward holding a mounted arcane ballista, faint blue glow from the weapon. At the front of the wagon, a girl figure drives the horse, her silhouette rim-lit in gold, hair whipped by wind. Warm amber side-backlight (5000K), volumetric light through dust, golden horizon, deep depth of field, 27mm lens, tracking dolly camera, cinematic grading, high-speed desperate chase, PBR rendering, motion blur on fast elements, slight camera shake.

## 镜头2[4-11s·9拍长镜头上半·节拍1-5] | 7秒视频

**参考图**：9宫格故事板整图
**景别**：从特写到中远景（动态变化）
**镜头**：一镜到底长镜头。从沙虫特写急速后拉→侧移→逼近马车
**打光**：暖金色（5000K）侧逆光，沙虫逆光暗色巨影，马车金色轮廓光，体积光穿透沙尘

**画面·9拍长镜头上半**：

**① 破土特写**：沙虫巨口从沙面爆裂而出，环形三排尖齿大张，沙粒飞溅，浅景深只有巨口清晰

**② 后拉揭示体量**：镜头急速后拉——黄褐色环形段状身体一节一节从沙中涌出，景深渐深

**③ 远景对比**：继续后拉——画面右侧远处马车出现，与沙虫体量形成巨大反差

**④ 侧移追踪**：镜头改为马车右侧侧移跟拍——沙虫从画面左侧向右猛追，马车右侧逃窜

**⑤ 逼近**：距离急速缩短——沙虫填满画面左侧，马车变大可辨识：棕色马+车厢+车尾窗口奥梵剪影

**站位**：沙虫@画面左侧由远及近；马车@画面右侧逐渐变大；奥梵@车尾窗口剪影
**背景虚化**：动态景深——①浅→②③渐深→④⑤中浅

### 正面提示词

Cinematic 7-second long take, continuous shot without cuts. BEAT 1: Extreme close-up — the sandworm's enormous circular maw bursts up from the desert floor, three rows of sharp teeth gaping, sand particles flying, shallow depth of field on the maw only. BEAT 2: Rapid pull-back along the sandworm's body — yellow-brown segmented body section by section rising from the sand, depth of field deepening. BEAT 3: Continued pull-back — a fleeing horse-drawn wagon appears in the distant right of frame, tiny compared to the sandworm which fills the left side like a moving hill. BEAT 4: Camera transitions to lateral tracking from the wagon's right side — the sandworm surges rightward in pursuit, the wagon racing ahead on the right. BEAT 5: Camera accelerates, closing the gap — the sandworm's body fills more of the frame, the wagon grows from a distant dot to a recognizable carriage with a brown horse, a girl figure silhouette at the rear window holding a weapon. Warm amber backlight (5000K), volumetric dust, golden rim light on the wagon, dynamic depth of field. Epic chase, single continuous take, PBR rendering, motion blur on fast elements.

### 负面提示词

static camera, multi-angle cuts, montage, close-up only, wide shot only, indoor, night, rain, urban, cartoon, anime, low quality, blurry, oversaturated, fish-eye, lens flare, chromatic aberration, wax figure, oily skin, watermark, text, duplicate characters, weapon pointing at camera, deep depth of field throughout, cat, feline, animal

### 音频描写

## 全局约束

PBR物理渲染，全局光照，次表面散射（皮肤），哑光金属材质（武器），粗麻布纹理（车厢），体积光，8K超高清，电影级调色（暖金+冷蓝对比），轻微胶片颗粒感。角色外貌全程一致。镜2+镜3为同一长镜头上下半，运动方向和构图必须连续，参考9宫格故事板。四镜整体节奏：大远景→长镜头9拍→中景人物反应，景别绝不重复。弩炮始终指向画面深处或窗口方向，绝不指向镜头。
